Johnston L.M.T

Welcome to the new massage style called Yomassage. Yomassage is a blend of six restorative poses during a 60 minute session with touch therapy. This new massage modality brings mind, body, and breath into a union aiding rest, stretch, and massage benefits. The advantage of Yomassage, is a fully-clothed group environment for the increased availability of self-care. The body is eased into relaxation using guided mindfulness messages, touch massage therapy, and gentle supported stretches. To schedule and save a spot in a Yomassage session, contact Renata Johnston at Muddy Lotus by email or text.
